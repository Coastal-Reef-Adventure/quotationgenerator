Terms & Conditions
01
Valid ID & Deposit
Renter must present 1 valid government ID or passport plus a refundable cash security deposit of ₱5,000 upon release.
02
Minimum Age
Driver must be at least 18 years old with a valid driver's license. International renters must present a passport and home-country license.
03
Fuel Policy
Units are released on a full tank and must be returned with a full tank. Refueling fees apply if returned with less.
04
Day Use Hours
Day Use rentals are 12 hours from pickup time, capped at 7:00 PM. All units must be returned by 7:00 PM regardless of pickup time.
05
Late Return
A 30-minute grace period applies. Returns beyond the grace period incur a late return charge of 10% of the daily rate per hour, rounded up.
06
Out-of-Office Hours Surcharge
Office hours are 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Requests outside these hours incur a ₱50/hour surcharge, rounded up.
07
Damage & Loss
Renter is liable for any damage, loss, or theft during the rental period. Repair costs will be deducted from the security deposit or billed directly.
08
Area Coverage
Units are for use within Siargao Island only. Off-island travel is strictly prohibited.
09
Prohibited Use
No racing, off-roading beyond agreed terrain, smoking inside the unit, or sub-renting to third parties. Violations forfeit the deposit.
10
Payment Terms
A 50% reservation fee is required to confirm your booking. Full payment is due upon pickup of the rental unit.
11
Cancellation & Rebooking
No cancellations and no refunds. Rebooking is allowed if requested at least 5 days before the scheduled rental date.
